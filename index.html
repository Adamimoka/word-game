Word Game




How to Play

1

Type a word that includes the given prompt. For example, if the prompt is "cat", you could type "cat", "caterpillar", or "application".

2

Press Enter to submit. Valid words earn points and reset the timer. Invalid words will not reset the timer.

3

The timer starts at 10 seconds and decreases over time. If you run out of time, the game ends.

4

Score as many points as you can by typing valid words before the timer runs out. Good luck!
